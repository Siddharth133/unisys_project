Who Are We?

We are a passionate team of students and Ml enthusiasts dedicated to improving patient outcomes through AI-powered innovation. Our mission is to revolutionize NHL diagnosis by providing pathologists with a powerful and reliable tool.

What We Do?

Traditional NHL diagnosis can be subjective and time-consuming. We address these challenges by providing a powerful and efficient solution. Leveraging Quantum Machine Learning technology, our model analyzes histopathological images with reliable accuracy, offering pathologists a valuable second opinion. This leads to earlier detection of NHL, allowing for better treatment options and improved patient outcomes. The platform seamlessly integrates into existing workflows, streamlining the diagnostic process and empowering pathologists to focus on patient care.

Why early detection matters?

Early detection of Non-Hodgkin Lymphoma is crucial as it significantly improves patient outcomes and survival rates. When detected at an early stage, the 5-year survival rate for NHL is around 73% globally and 80% in India , compared to only 57% and 40% respectively when diagnosed at an advanced stage. Early-stage NHL is more treatable, with patients having a higher chance of achieving complete remission and better long-term progression-free survival. Moreover, early detection reduces the economic burden on patients, families, and healthcare systems, as treatment costs for advanced NHL can be up to three times higher than early-stage disease in India. With NHL being the 7th most common cancer globally and accounting for 2.2% of all cancers in India, effective early detection methods like our Quantum Machine Learning approach can potentially save countless lives and improve overall NHL management.
